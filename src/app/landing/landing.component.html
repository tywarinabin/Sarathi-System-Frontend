What are the benefits of RAG systems?

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems combine information retrieval with generative AI to provide accurate, contextually relevant responses. Benefits include improved accuracy, reduced hallucinations, and access to up-to-date information.

How does Sarathi implement RAG?

Sarathi uses advanced vector embeddings and semantic search to retrieve relevant documents, which are then fed to our language model for generation. This ensures responses are both intelligent and grounded in your data.
